Superagent helps developers make their AI apps safe. The Safety Agent exposes three methods — Guard, Redact, and Analyze — that protect AI applications in real time.

## Capabilities

  <IconCard
    title="Guard"

    icon={<Shield className="size-4" />}
  />
  <IconCard
    title="Redact"

    icon={<EyeOff className="size-4" />}
  />
  <IconCard
    title="Analyze"

    icon={<Check className="size-4" />}
  />
</Cards>

## Why Superagent

- **Developer-first**: Built for teams shipping AI apps
- **Works with any model**: Choose based on latency, accuracy, cost, or compliance
- **Protects where apps fail**: Inputs, outputs, tool calls, and intermediate steps
- **Proven performance**: Up to 30% better Guard performance on the BrowseSafe benchmark
